# Scores24 Scraper - Prompts for AI Assistant

Copy and paste these prompts exactly into the chat whenever you want me to use the scraper to find specific insights.

---

### 1. 🎯 The Locks (High Confidence Pick Filter)
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[LEAGUE]** on **[YYYY-MM-DD]**. I only want you to give me 'The Locks'. Only output a simple table containing games where the site's official **Confidence is 80% or higher**. Do not show me games with lower confidence."

### 2. 🏛️ Public Consensus (User Voting Splits)
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[LEAGUE]** on **[YYYY-MM-DD]**. I am looking for the heaviest public consensus. Show me a table of ONLY the games where the User Vote is highly skewed (e.g., **75%+ vs 25%-**). Include the total number of votes cast so I can see sample size."

### 3. 🚨 The Hype Detector (Suggestive Language)
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[LEAGUE]** on **[YYYY-MM-DD]**. I want to know which writers are emotionally invested. Print a table of all the games, but put a massive warning emoji 🚨 next to any game where the scraper detected **suggestive language** (e.g., lock, safe bet, guaranteed). Include the exact quote it found."

### 4. 📈 Deep Historical Trends (Stat Parsing)
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[LEAGUE]** on **[YYYY-MM-DD]**. I am looking for severe historical trends. Scan the 'Stat trends' for all matches and ONLY show me games where one team has **lost or not won a specific period/half in their last 8+ games**. Show the match, the tip, and precisely what that specific trend bullet point was."

### 5. 🗓️ The General Sweep (Standard Fetch)
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[LEAGUE]** on **[YYYY-MM-DD]**. Give me a single, clean markdown table with just the 'Our Choice' top tip, the Odds, and the Confidence for every single game on that date."

### 6. ⚔️ Specific Matchup Check
> "Run the Scores24 scraper for **[SPORT/LEAGUE]** but ONLY look for the specific matchup: **[TEAM A vs TEAM B]**. Show me the exact odds, tip, user vote, and trends for this specific game."
